Who We Are

A community built for every Oregon Tech student.

The Oregon Tech AI Club brings together students passionate about artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and information technology — regardless of major or prior experience. We focus on practical learning, ethical technology, career preparation, and student-led projects.

Membership is free and open to all enrolled Oregon Tech students. Join through The Roost or stop by a meeting to get connected.
