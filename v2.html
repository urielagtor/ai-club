Who We Are

A community built for every Oregon Tech student.

The Oregon Tech AI Club brings together students interested in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, software, data, and emerging technology. Whether you are new to AI or already building technical projects, the club provides a place to learn, collaborate, and grow.

Membership is free and open to all enrolled Oregon Tech students. Join through The Roost or attend a meeting to get connected.

No Dues Membership is free for enrolled students
All Majors Beginner-friendly and open to every major
Project-Based Learn through workshops, demos, and projects
