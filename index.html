// Who We Are

AI, Cybersecurity,
& IT — United

The Oregon Tech AI Club builds a welcoming, interdisciplinary community for students interested in AI and its intersection with cybersecurity and IT — regardless of major or prior experience. We offer hands-on labs, workshops, and projects that develop practical, industry-ready skills.

Membership is free and open to all currently enrolled Oregon Tech students. Join us on The Roost or sign in at any meeting to get started.

// What We Do

Focus Areas

We balance activities across three pillars — AI, Cybersecurity, and IT — with hands-on labs, competitions, speaker events, and real projects.

🤖

Artificial Intelligence

Foundational AI, machine learning, data science, and automation. Build and train real models — from regression to deep neural networks.

✨

Generative AI

Explore large language models, image generation, prompt engineering, and responsible deployment of GenAI tools in real-world contexts.

🔒

Cybersecurity

Network security, application security, ethical hacking (within legal limits), incident response, and AI-powered cyber defense strategies.

🖧

IT & Infrastructure

Networking, cloud computing, systems administration, and IT best practices. Bridge the gap between AI and the infrastructure that runs it.

⚖️

Ethics & Responsible AI

Tackle bias, fairness, transparency, and safety. We promote ethical use of AI and security technologies in alignment with professional standards.

🚀

Professional Development

Industry speakers, alumni networking, competitions, certifications, and project showcases to support your academic and career growth.
