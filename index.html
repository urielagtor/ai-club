Who We Are

A Community Built for Every Student

The Oregon Tech AI Club brings together students passionate about artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and information technology — regardless of major or prior experience. We're a welcoming, interdisciplinary community focused on building real skills for real careers.

Our activities span hands-on labs, workshops, guest speaker sessions, competitions, and collaborative projects. Whether you're just starting out or already deep in the field, there's a place for you here.

Membership is free and open to all enrolled Oregon Tech students. Join us on The Roost or sign in at any general meeting.

What We Do

Focus Areas

We balance activities across three pillars — AI, Cybersecurity, and IT — with hands-on projects, competitions, and industry connections.

🤖

Artificial Intelligence

Foundational AI, machine learning, data science, and automation. Build and train real models — from regression to deep neural networks.

✨

Generative AI

Explore large language models, image generation, prompt engineering, and responsible deployment of GenAI tools in real-world contexts.

🔒

Cybersecurity

Network security, application security, ethical hacking, incident response, and AI-powered cyber defense strategies.

🖧

IT & Infrastructure

Networking, cloud computing, systems administration, and IT best practices. Bridge the gap between AI and the infrastructure that runs it.

⚖️

Ethics & Responsible AI

Tackle bias, fairness, transparency, and safety. We promote ethical use of AI and security technologies in alignment with professional standards.

🚀

Professional Development

Industry speakers, alumni networking, competitions, certifications, and project showcases to support your academic and career growth.
